About Me

About Page Responses

A) The purpose of this website is to advertise the electricial services of Minuteman Electric. They are able to perform any electricial needs including residential, industrial, and commericial work. I made sure to talk a little bit about the services that can be provided with estimated prices, provided photos of some finished projects, shown some reviews, and included the contact information.

B) My focus on this website was to make it easy to use for the user while providing sufficient information about the electrical company. On every page I kept the header the same with the logo, phone number, and the pages list. I also tried to keep a similar text format and coloring for all the pages. I focused on using a light blue with red and included these colors as boxes around the text with a white background. This helped the text stand out and made it easy to read. On the home page, I thought it was important to put a face on the company so I used my "uncle's" picture at work. I also wanted to include some quick information about the comapny, but I kept it short and tried spreading it out across the page to make it more appealing. I also selected a finished project that I thought was visiually appealing to show off the work. I lastly included a video that is a commerical of the company (actually a SNHU commericial). The about us page talks a little bit about my uncle and his background. I also included a place for the mission statement. I thought it was important for the user to see who my uncle is along with his experience and the company's mission. Since this is a veteran owned company, I wanted to include a picture of our US Navy. My main focus on the services page was showing some of the common services with an estimated price. I decided to put this in a table to keep it organized and easy to read. The Finished projects page is focused on being visually appealing, so I did not include any information, just photos of the work itself. On the reviews / testimonials page, I included a couple reviews from clients as well as added a form that can be completed by the user to leave a review. Finally the contact us page is short and simple. I wanted to provide the user with the contact information as well as the company hours. I kept the text short to make the focus on the contact information.

C) My uncle actually does own his own electrician business, so I could potentially update this site for him to use in the future if he wished. I'm not sure that I would add any pages, but one thing that was bothering me was the fact that my footer was moving around the page a bit. I would like it to stay at the bottom, but wasn't able to figure out exactly how to do that. A real company logo I believe would have helped it look more professional. I would like to set up the form on the testimonial page to actually work and leave reviews that can be viewed on the page. With some javascript, I believe that would be possible. Overall, I like the site I have created, but it doesn't look professional enough for me. I can't exactly pinpoint any one thing, but something about the overall site just isn't up to the standard I would like. I'm hoping as I create more sites, that I am able to get closer to my goal.
